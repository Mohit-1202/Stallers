What inspires us doing it ?

Why?

One of our co-founders came from a modest background with parents who were dedicated to his education and future success. Despite their support, he struggled with immense pressure and couldn't achieve high marks in his exams. At that time, he alone was blamed for the failure, making it feel like the end for him. But trust me, it wasn't entirely his fault the system is designed in a way that sets people up for failure.
Now, he understands what went wrong.
We don't want any innocent person to endure such hardship.

What really went wrong?

Problem

He enrolled in classes and began attending, but without proper guidance and self-control, he soon started missing them frequently. With no one else to blame, he eventually spiraled into self-doubt, which could have shattered his ambitions.
Not every one is lucky some people are consumed by self-doubt and lose their way.
We don't want your child to experience the same.
